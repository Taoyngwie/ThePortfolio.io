The Bicycle for earth and Sustainability

What is this game :

This game is a game. Players must ride their bicycles to collect electrical energy and use them as points to conserve natural resources and the planet in accordance with The trend of sustainability is coming in the concept of preserving the world's nature.

Why this game :

The origin and importance of this game was developed to be used in competitions for hackathon competition It only lasts for 3 days and developers have to create a project to match the theme to compete in the event. Our team has created an electric cycling game.

HOW :

This game will work by having the player play on an electric bicycle in a fitness center or anywhere and there will be sensors to detect or process images and then display them within the game for use in calculations. How long will the player travel and how much electricity can be stored? Because the electric connector is connected to the movement of the player when cycling to use electricity in emergencies or can be used to reduce future expenses.
